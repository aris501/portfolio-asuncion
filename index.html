Aris Asuncion

3d Printing, Scale figure enthusiast, and Toy Photographer

Prior to the pandemic, Aris worked on scaled down model production with the Baguio city based fabrications Lab of Saint Louis University - College of Architectue. Also known as SIRIB. He specializes in data acquisition, digital sculpting, and 3d printing. His passion for sci-fi pop culture, anime, and an ever growing collection of different scales of statues and action figures has influenced him to register as a toy store sole proprietor with the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI). Admitedly, he has since realized and acknowledged how much he lacks in advertising skills, business management, knowledge / tools in photography and web development. His first step, then, to address these inadequacies was to take up this full-stack course with Zuitt. He hopes to keep up with his peers in this fast paced learning environment and wishes that he would be able to use whatever he'll learn to complement his humble business.
